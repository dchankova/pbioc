About "Physiotherapy Bio Center"

The "Physiotherapy Bio Center" was created to treat, restore, maintain and care for the physical health of our clients. The center is qualified in Massage Therapy (Physiotherapy) and Physical Medicine. We have the latest generation of high quality equipment for physiotherapy (electrotherapy, Ultrasound therapy, Deep oscillation) from the German company Physiomed, which is a leading manufacturer of equipment for the rehabilitation. All our products including massage oils are organic in origin.

With us you can count on professionalism and the highest quality.
